How AI Upcycling Works

Transforming your old clothes is as entirely guided by our intelligent Vision LLM. Here's your journey from waste to wearable.

1. Snap & Upload

Take 3 clear photos of your old garment: one of the front, one of the back, and a close-up highlighting the fabric texture or any damages.

2. AI Analysis

Qwen Vision LLM instantly scans your uploads. It securely identifies the garment type, evaluates the material probability, and assesses its condition.

3. Generate Ideas

You get three tailored project ideas categorized by difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard). Pick the one that fits your skill level!

4. Do It Yourself!

Clicking on an idea generates a comprehensive, step-by-step instruction guide so you can cut, stitch, and upcycle your garment perfectly.
